Complete Feature Set

Everything you need built in. From gaming to creation to AI, ForgeOS is fully equipped.

🎮 Gaming Stack

Steam & Proton

Play thousands of games with Steam and Proton compatibility layer. Run Windows games natively on Linux.

Lutris

Universal gaming platform for managing and launching games from multiple sources with optimized configurations.

MangoHud

In-game overlay to monitor performance metrics including FPS, GPU/CPU usage, and temperatures.

Gamemode

Automatic performance optimization daemon that adjusts system settings for optimal gaming performance.

🎨 Content Creation Tools

Blender 3D

Professional 3D modeling, animation, rendering, and visual effects creation suite.

OBS Studio

Streaming and recording software for content creators. Broadcasting to multiple platforms simultaneously.

Kdenlive

Non-linear video editor with advanced effects, transitions, and color grading capabilities.

Audio Suite

Ardour, Audacity, and LMMS for music production, audio editing, and beat making.

⚙️ System Features

Forge Desktop

Custom desktop environment with visual effects. Dark charcoal background with cyan accents. Fully intuitive.

Forge App Center

Curated software library with one-click installation. Discover and manage applications easily.

Forge Control Panel

Single location for all system settings. Display, audio, network, power management, and user accounts.

Terminal Optional

Fully usable without command line. Every setting and operation through graphical interface.

Four Editions, Every Use Case

ForgeOS adapts to your hardware and needs. From powerful gaming rigs to older hardware, there's a ForgeOS edition for you.

FLAGSHIP

ForgeOS Pro

The complete experience for creators and gamers

  • Processor: Octa-core recommended
  • Memory: 16 GB or more
  • Storage: 100 GB NVMe SSD
  • Graphics: RTX 3070 or better

Full desktop, gaming stack, creation tools, fAI with all features enabled.

EVERYDAY

ForgeOS Home

For families and everyday users

  • Processor: Dual-core
  • Memory: 4 GB minimum
  • Storage: 25 GB
  • Includes: Firefox, LibreOffice

Clean, fast desktop with parental controls and family account management.

EDUCATION

ForgeOS Edu

Purpose-built for classrooms

  • Processor: Dual-core
  • Memory: 2 GB minimum
  • Storage: 15 GB
  • Free: For all schools

Classroom management, exam mode, Python, Scratch, and Arduino IDE built-in.

LIGHTWEIGHT

ForgeOS Lite

For older computers and limited hardware

  • Processor: Single-core
  • Memory: 1 GB minimum
  • Storage: 10 GB
  • RAM Usage: Under 300 MB idle

Run from USB stick. Essential tools with minimal resource footprint.

Introducing fAI

Exclusive to ForgeOS Pro: Local, private artificial intelligence. All processing happens on your hardware. No external servers. No data transmission.

Complete Privacy Guaranteed

Every fAI feature processes data locally on your computer. Your conversations, images, code, and documents never leave your system. You have full control.

  • fAI Chat

    Local language model with graphical chat interface. Conversation, writing, coding questions, and research assistance.

  • fAI Vision

    Image generation, editing, inpainting, and upscaling. Accessible directly from the right-click menu in file manager and desktop.

  • fAI Voice

    Speech-to-text dictation that works in any text field. Text-to-speech with natural voices. Fully offline operation.

  • fAI Code

    Code assistant integrated with system text editor. Autocomplete suggestions, error detection, and refactoring help.

  • fAI Translate

    Offline translation for text and documents. Covers multiple languages without requiring internet connection.

  • Flexible Model Management

    Download and run different open models through the fAI interface. No specific models bundled. Full user control.

  • Hardware Requirements

    fAI Chat: 4GB VRAM. fAI Vision: 6GB VRAM. Voice, Code, Translate: No additional requirements.

Why Local AI Matters

Cloud-based AI services collect your data, train on your inputs, and depend on internet connectivity. fAI is different. Everything stays private. Everything stays fast. Everything stays under your control.
