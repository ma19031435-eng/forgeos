About ForgeOS

ForgeOS represents a bold vision for the future of operating systems. Built from the ground up with modern principles, it combines the power and flexibility of open-source development with the polished experience users expect from contemporary systems.

Whether you're a seasoned developer, a creative professional, or someone who simply wants more control over their computing environment, ForgeOS provides the tools and foundation you need to succeed.

Our mission is to democratize access to powerful, secure, and customizable operating systems while fostering a vibrant community of contributors and users who believe in the power of open-source software.
