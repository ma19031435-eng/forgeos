Four Editions, Every Use Case

ForgeOS adapts to your hardware and needs. From powerful gaming rigs to older hardware, there's a ForgeOS edition for you.

FLAGSHIP

ForgeOS Pro

The complete experience for creators and gamers

  • Processor: Octa-core recommended
  • Memory: 16 GB or more
  • Storage: 100 GB NVMe SSD
  • Graphics: RTX 3070 or better

Full desktop, gaming stack, creation tools, fAI with all features.

EVERYDAY

ForgeOS Home

For families and everyday users

  • Processor: Dual-core
  • Memory: 4 GB minimum
  • Storage: 25 GB
  • Includes: Firefox, LibreOffice

Clean, fast desktop for daily tasks. Includes parental controls.

EDUCATION

ForgeOS Edu

Purpose-built for classrooms

  • Processor: Dual-core
  • Memory: 2 GB minimum
  • Storage: 15 GB
  • Free: For all schools

Classroom management, locked exam mode, Python, Scratch, Arduino.

LIGHTWEIGHT

ForgeOS Lite

For older computers and limited hardware

  • Processor: Single-core
  • Memory: 1 GB minimum
  • Storage: 10 GB
  • RAM Usage: Under 300 MB idle

Run from USB stick. Essential tools for everyone.

Meet fAI

Exclusive to ForgeOS Pro: Local, private artificial intelligence. All processing happens on your hardware. No external servers. No data transmission.

Complete Privacy

Every fAI feature processes data locally on your computer. Your conversations, images, code, and documents never leave your system.

  • fAI Chat

    Local language model with graphical chat interface. Conversation, writing, coding questions, research.

  • fAI Vision

    Image generation, editing, inpainting, and upscaling. Accessible from the right-click menu.

  • fAI Voice

    Speech-to-text dictation in any text field. Text-to-speech with natural voices. Fully offline.

  • fAI Code

    Code assistant in your text editor. Autocomplete suggestions, error detection, refactoring help.

  • fAI Translate

    Offline translation for text and documents. Multiple languages without internet connection.

  • Flexible Models

    Download and run different open models through the fAI interface. User controlled, not bundled.

  • Hardware Requirements

    fAI Chat needs 4GB VRAM. fAI Vision needs 6GB VRAM. Voice, Code, and Translate have no additional requirements.
